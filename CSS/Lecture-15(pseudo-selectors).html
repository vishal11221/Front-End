Pseudo Selectors:- Pseudo-selectors in CSS are special keywords that allow you to style elements based on their state, position, or interaction without modifying the HTML. They are categorized into two main types:-

  1. Pseudo-Classes:- It is used to style elements based on their state, position, or interaction.
  2. Pseudo-Elements:- It is used to style elements based on their position or content.
